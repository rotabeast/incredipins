Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Incredipins ("the Application") collects, uses, and handles information when you use the application.

1. Who We Are

Incredipins is a personal-use Pinterest content scheduling and publishing tool. It is operated by an individual developer and is not a commercial product.

2. Information We Collect

To function, Incredipins collects only what is necessary:

  • Pinterest OAuth Token – An access token issued by Pinterest when you authorise the app. This is used solely to publish pins on your behalf.
  • Pinterest Profile Info – Basic account details (username, profile ID) returned by Pinterest during authentication.
  • Pin Content – Titles, descriptions, image URLs, and links that you enter into the application for the purpose of publishing.

3. How We Use Your Information

All information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with the Pinterest API on your behalf
  • Create and publish pins to your Pinterest account at scheduled times
  • Display your Pinterest boards within the application

We do not use your information for any other purpose.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. The only external service your data is sent to is Pinterest, via their official API, as required to provide the core functionality of the app.

5. Data Storage

Pin content and scheduling data is stored locally within the application's own database, which you control. Pinterest access tokens are stored securely and are not transmitted to any party other than Pinterest's API servers.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you use the application. You may delete pins, boards, or all data at any time from within the application.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data held about you within the application
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Revoke Pinterest API access at any time via your Pinterest account settings

8. Third-Party Services

Incredipins uses the Pinterest API. Your use of Pinterest is governed by Pinterest's own Privacy Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the Incredipins GitHub repository or email scwbuncrana@gmail.com.
